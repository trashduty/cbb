# Totals Analysis: Regressed vs Unregressed Comparison

## Methodology

- **Model combination**: Mean (average) of selected model totals
- **Regressed**: p_final = 0.5 × lookup_prob + 0.5 × 0.50, edge = p_final - 52.38%
- **Unregressed**: edge = lookup_prob - 52.38%
- **Bet direction**: Both (positive edge = bet over/under, negative = bet opposite)
- **ROI**: Standard -110 juice
- **Filter**: Home team only (one bet per game)
